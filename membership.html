Why Join the League?

Membership in the Winston-Salem Barrel League offers more than access to exceptional spirits—it's an invitation to a community of like-minded enthusiasts, exclusive experiences, and lasting friendships.

Member Benefits

01

Monthly Tastings

Attend expertly curated monthly tastings featuring bourbon, whiskey, and other exceptional spirits. Each session includes guided tasting notes, educational content, and thoughtful discussion led by knowledgeable members.

02

Exclusive Barrel Picks

First access to our private barrel selections. Members have the opportunity to participate in barrel selection trips and purchase bottles from our club picks—unique expressions you won't find anywhere else.

03

Distillery Excursions

Join organized trips to renowned distilleries throughout Kentucky and the Southeast. Experience bourbon at its source with behind-the-scenes tours, private tastings, and insider access.

04

Members-Only Forum

Connect with fellow enthusiasts through our private online community. Share tasting notes, discuss new releases, organize bottle shares, and build lasting friendships beyond our monthly gatherings.

05

Educational Resources

Access our growing library of tasting guides, distillery profiles, and educational materials. Learn from guest speakers, master distillers, and industry experts at special events throughout the year.

06

Community & Networking

Build genuine connections with fellow spirit enthusiasts in Winston-Salem. Our members range from curious newcomers to seasoned collectors, all united by a passion for quality spirits and good company.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a bourbon expert to join?

Absolutely not! We welcome enthusiasts at all experience levels, from those just beginning their journey to seasoned collectors. Our community is built on shared curiosity and mutual respect.

How often do you meet?

We host monthly tasting events, typically on the third Saturday evening. Additional special events, distillery trips, and social gatherings are scheduled throughout the year.

Can I bring a guest to events?

Members may bring one guest to regular monthly tastings (age 21+). Some exclusive events and distillery trips are members-only. Guest attendance helps prospective members experience our community before joining.

What if I can't attend every event?

We understand life gets busy. While we hope you'll attend as many events as possible, there's no attendance requirement. Your membership benefits remain active regardless of how many events you attend.

How do barrel picks work?

Several times per year, we select private barrels from distilleries. Members are notified when new picks are available and can purchase bottles at competitive pricing. Higher membership tiers receive priority or guaranteed allocation.

Can I cancel my membership?

Memberships are annual commitments. While we don't offer refunds for early cancellation, you can choose not to renew when your term ends. We're confident you'll want to stay once you experience our community.
